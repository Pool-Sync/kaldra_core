# Cultural Vectors (48) - Kindra Engine

This document details the 48 cultural vectors used by the Kindra Engine to analyze text across three layers: Cultural Macro (L1), Semiotic Media (L2), and Structural Systemic (L3).

## Overview

The 48 vectors are distributed as follows:
- **Layer 1 (Cultural Macro)**: 16 vectors
- **Layer 2 (Semiotic Media)**: 16 vectors
- **Layer 3 (Structural Systemic)**: 16 vectors

Each vector represents a specific cultural force, tension, or pattern.

## Integration with Kindra Engine

These vectors are scored by the Kindra Engine (Rule-Based, LLM, or Hybrid) and fed into the TW369 Engine to calculate drift and the Δ144 Engine to determine the system state.

- **L1 Vectors** primarily influence **TW Plane 9 (Metanoia)** and **Δ144 Transcendent** states.
- **L2 Vectors** primarily influence **TW Plane 3 (Action)** and **Δ144 Expansive** states.
- **L3 Vectors** primarily influence **TW Plane 6 (Structure)** and **Δ144 Contractive** states.

## Known Limitations

*   **Western Bias**: The current 48 vectors are heavily influenced by Western cultural dynamics.
*   **Static Definitions**: The vectors are fixed in JSON and do not evolve automatically.
*   **Overlap**: Some vectors (e.g., E03 Trust vs. E04 Decay) have significant semantic overlap.
*   **Binary Polarity**: The current system treats polarity as a simple spectrum, missing orthogonal dimensions.
